ARCHITECTURE
================================================================================

Database Tables:
- absensi (attendance sessions)
  - kelas_perkuliahan_id, pertemuan_ke, tanggal, jam_mulai, jam_selesai
  - session_status (draft/buka/tutup)
  - rangkuman, berita_acara, catatan
  - waktu_buka, waktu_tutup

- absensi_mahasiswa (student attendance records)
  - absensi_id, mahasiswa_id, status (hadir/izin/sakit)

Routes:
