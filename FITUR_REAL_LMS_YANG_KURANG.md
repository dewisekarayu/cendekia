# 🎓 FITUR REAL LMS (Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle, Google Classroom) YANG KURANG DI CENDEKIA

Ini bukan orang yang diminta, tapi yang **REAL LMS enterprise punya** tapi Cendekia belum ada!

---

## 🔴 CRITICAL FEATURES (LMS WAJIB PUNYA)

### **1. LEARNING PATH / COURSE STRUCTURE** ❌
**Canvas/Moodle punya:**
- Module/unit dengan urutan (Module 1 → Module 2 → Module 3)
- Prerequisite: "Harus beres Module 1 sebelum buka Module 2"
- Progress tracking: "Student sudah 60% selesai course ini"

**Cendekia:** Ga ada struktur. Material, tugas, quiz semua campur.

**Analogi:**
- Canvas: Seperti buku dengan chapter terstruktur (Ch 1 → Ch 2 → Ch 3)
- Cendekia: Seperti tumpukan kertas, mahasiswa harus sorting sendiri

**Impact:** Students bingung mulai dari mana. Teachers ga bisa enforce alur pembelajaran.

---

### **2. QUIZ / TEST SYSTEM** ❌ (HUGE!)
**Canvas/Blackboard punya:**
- Multiple choice, essay, fill-blank, matching questions
- Auto-grading untuk objective questions
- Question bank (bisa reuse pertanyaan di berbagai quiz)
- Randomized questions per student (biar ga cheating)
- Time limit per quiz
- Show/hide answers setelah submit
- Detailed analytics: "80% students menjawab #3 salah"

**Cendekia:** Ga ada quiz system sama sekali!

**Analogi:**
- Canvas: Seperti punya automated test system (seperti LKS-UN)
- Cendekia: Ga ada, harus manual buat test di paper

**Impact:** Teachers harus bikin & grade assessment manually. Students ga bisa practice test online. No auto-grading.

**This is MASSIVE buat LMS!**

---

### **3. GRADEBOOK (ADVANCED)** ❌
**Canvas/Moodle punya:**
- Weighted categories: "Quiz 30%, Assignment 40%, Participation 30%"
- Automatic grade calculation
- Grade breakdown per assignment
- Class average comparison ("You got 85, class average 78")
- Grade trends graph ("Your grade going up/down")
- What-if analysis ("Kalo UAS saya dapet 90, final grade berapa?")
- Excused absences / incomplete grades

**Cendekia:** Simple numeric grades. No weighted calculation. No trends. No analysis.

**Impact:** Teachers pusing calculate weighted grades manually. Students ga bisa liat progress trend.

---

### **4. RUBRIC / GRADING CRITERIA** ❌
**Canvas/Blackboard punya:**
- Rubric linked ke assignments
- Criteria points: "Creativity (5pts), Accuracy (3pts), Presentation (2pts)"
- Performance levels: "Excellent, Good, Fair, Poor"
- Comments template per criteria
- Student bisa liat rubric sebelum submit
- Auto-calculate based on checked boxes

**Cendekia:** No rubric system.

**Impact:** Grading subjective. Students ga tahu ekspektasi exact apa.

---

### **5. PLAGIARISM DETECTION** ❌
**Canvas/Moodle punya:**
- Turnitin integration (check copy-paste)
- Similarity report
- Flag high-plagiarism submissions
- Student can check before submit

**Cendekia:** No plagiarism checking.

**Impact:** Copy-paste gampang ga ketahuan.

---

### **6. SUBMISSION TRACKING & REATTEMPT** ❌
**Canvas/Blackboard punya:**
- Submission history (siapa submit kapan, berapa kali)
- Late submission flag & policy
- Allow re-submission dengan grade replacement/keeping best grade
- Submission vs. grade view

**Cendekia:** Only current submission. No history. No re-attempt allowed.

**Impact:** Teachers ga bisa liat who submitted late. No re-attempt encouragement.

---

### **7. PEER REVIEW / PEER GRADING** ❌
**Canvas/Moodle punya:**
- Students grade assignment dari students lain
- Anonymous grading
- Rubric-based peer feedback
- Multiple reviewers per submission

**Cendekia:** Teacher-only grading.

**Impact:** Hilang collaborative learning aspect. Teachers overloaded dengan grading.

---

### **8. CALENDAR / CENTRALIZED SCHEDULE** ❌
**Canvas/Moodle punya:**
- Unified calendar (assignment deadlines, class meets, exams, events)
- iCal export (Google Calendar, Outlook sync)
- Notification reminders
- Conflict detection

**Cendekia:** Schedule separate dari assignment. No calendar view.

**Impact:** Students ga liat semua deadlines in one place.

---

### **9. INBOX / MESSAGING SYSTEM** ❌
**Canvas/Blackboard punya:**
- Direct messaging teacher-student
- Group messaging
- Notification center
- Compose to multiple recipients
- Folders & search

**Cendekia:** No direct messaging. Forum aja.

**Impact:** Students harus di forum buat tanya dosen (not private).

---

### **10. ATTENDANCE / ROSTER** ❌
**Canvas/Blackboard punya:**
- Class roster dengan photo
- Attendance tracking
- Roll call / check-in feature
- Export attendance report
- Sync dengan section enrollment

**Cendekia:** Presensi ada tapi ga detail roster tracking.

**Impact:** Teachers ga bisa quick view siapa present/absent.

---
